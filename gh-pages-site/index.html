Core Features

Everything a campus canteen needs.

From real-time stock checks to AI-generated daily kitchen reports — built end-to-end with Next.js, Firebase, and Gemini.

restaurant_menu

Live Menu & Stock

Real-time inventory count with optimistic locking. Students see exact stock before ordering — no more "sold out" surprises at the counter.

qr_code_2

UPI Pre-Order Checkout

Generates a dynamic UPI QR code. AI auto-verifies the payment receipt screenshot. Falls back to manual admin review on failure.

conveyor_belt

Parallel Kitchen Queue

Kitchen staff track each item's prep status independently. Orders auto-advance to "Ready" when all items complete — eliminating paper token chaos.

smart_toy

Gemini AI Insights

Daily cron aggregates student reviews via Gemini Flash. Kitchen managers receive actionable bullet-point summaries every evening automatically.

account_balance_wallet

Campus Wallet

Students top up a campus credit wallet. Orders are atomically deducted and confirmed instantly — no UPI flow required for repeat users.

verified_user

OTP-Verified Registration

Email OTP verifies real inboxes during sign-up. Gmail canonical normalization blocks alias spam. Admins approve or reject pending registrations.
